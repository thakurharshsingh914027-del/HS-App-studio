Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HS APP STUDIO collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile applications, websites, and related services.

Information We Collect

Depending on the app or service you use, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide directly, such as messages sent to support or feedback forms.
  • Device and app information, such as device model, operating system version, app version, language, crash logs, diagnostics, and performance data.
  • Usage information, such as app interactions, feature usage, and approximate session activity.
  • Advertising or analytics identifiers when an app uses ads, analytics, or measurement tools.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate our apps, provide support, improve performance, fix bugs, protect against misuse, understand app usage, comply with legal obligations, and show ads when applicable.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, hosting, crash reporting, or app distribution. These providers may collect information according to their own privacy policies. Examples may include Google Play services, Firebase, AdMob, or similar mobile service providers when integrated into a specific app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate our apps, when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or as part of a business transfer involving HS APP STUDIO.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13 unless a specific app states otherwise. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Choices

You may limit certain data collection through your device settings, app permissions, advertising ID controls, or by uninstalling the app. You may also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information where required by applicable law.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. By using our apps or services, you understand that information may be transferred and processed where we or our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at contact@hsappstudio.com.
